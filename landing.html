The Kawasaki W175 BS6 2023 is a retro-style motorcycle that packs a punch with its 177.00 cc 4 air-cooled, 4-stroke single-cylinder engine. The semi-digital retro-style speedometer adds to the vintage feel of the bike, while the Ebony and Candy Persimmon Red color options offer a distinct and stylish appearance. Overall, the Kawasaki W175 BS6 2023 is an excellent choice for riders looking for a reliable and stylish motorcycle that offers a unique riding experience.
